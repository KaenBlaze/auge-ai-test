# Campaign Advertising Calendar (CAL-2026-001)

## Section 3 — Electoral Silence and Advertising Blackout

The advertising blackout begins at 00:00 on the calendar day that is **72 hours before election day** and ends when polls close. During the blackout:

- Paid persuasion advertising is prohibited
- Mass SMS campaigning is prohibited
- Sponsored political calls are prohibited

This calendar implements the electoral silence requirements of LEY-2021-014 Article 31.

## Section 8 — Digital Advertising Archives

All digital political advertisements must be archived for **24 months** from first publication. Archives must retain creative assets, placement metadata, spend records, and targeting parameters sufficient for audit reconstruction.
