# Republic of Valdoria Electoral Code (LEY-2021-014)

## Article 31 — Electoral Silence Period

The electoral silence period is the final **72 hours before election day**. During this period the following activities are prohibited:

- Paid persuasion advertising in any medium
- Mass SMS campaigning
- Sponsored political calls

Public institutions must publish reminders of the silence period no later than seven days before election day. Violations are subject to administrative fines and referral to the Electoral Integrity Office.

## Article 32 — Permitted Communications During Silence

Factual notifications about polling locations, accessibility accommodations, and emergency service interruptions remain permitted when they contain no persuasive content.
