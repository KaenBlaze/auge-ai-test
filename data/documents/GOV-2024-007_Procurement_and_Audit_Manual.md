# Procurement and Audit Manual (GOV-2024-007)

## Section 9 — Procurement Audit Record Fields

Procurement audit records must include, at minimum:

- Contract identifier
- Vendor legal name and tax ID
- Award date
- Line-item descriptions
- Unit price and total amount
- Approving officer
- Supporting bid evaluation memo reference
- Payment authorization reference

Incomplete records cannot be closed during annual compliance review.

## Section 10 — Retention

Procurement audit records must be retained for seven years unless a longer period is specified by sector regulation.
