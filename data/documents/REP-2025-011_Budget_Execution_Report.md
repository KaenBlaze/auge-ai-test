# Budget Execution Report (REP-2025-011)

## Section 2 — Executive Summary

National program execution for fiscal year 2025 remained within aggregate appropriation limits. Education and health outlays increased in all regions, with Central Region receiving the largest absolute increases.

## Section 6 — Reallocation Activity

Three programs triggered Section 14 reallocation review during 2025. All reallocations above 15% received Budget Committee approval and published rationales as required by REP-2025-010.
