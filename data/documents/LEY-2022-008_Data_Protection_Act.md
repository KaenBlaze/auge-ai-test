# Data Protection and Sovereignty Act (LEY-2022-008)

## Article 18 — Biometric Data Residency

Biometric data collected by Valdorian public institutions must remain physically and logically inside the Republic of Valdoria. Such data **may not be stored, processed, backed up, or mirrored in foreign cloud infrastructure**.

Agencies must maintain an auditable chain of custody for biometric enrollment, verification, and deletion events. Cross-border transfers require an explicit statutory exemption published in the national gazette.

## Article 19 — Encryption and Access Controls

Biometric templates must be encrypted at rest using approved national cryptographic modules. Access is limited to role-based accounts with quarterly recertification.
