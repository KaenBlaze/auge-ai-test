# Budget Transparency Guidelines (REP-2025-010)

## Section 14 — Budget Reallocations

A budget reallocation above **15%** of an approved program budget requires:

1. Budget Committee approval
2. A published rationale describing the fiscal necessity and expected service impact

Reallocations at or below 15% may be executed by the program director with monthly reporting to the Budget Committee. Emergency reallocations above 15% require retroactive committee review within ten business days.

## Section 15 — Public Disclosure

Approved reallocations above 15% must be posted to the open budget portal within five business days of approval.
