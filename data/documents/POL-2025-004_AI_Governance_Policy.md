# Public AI Governance Policy (POL-2025-004)

## Section 5 — AI-Generated Recommendations

Every AI-generated recommendation presented to a decision-maker must include:

- Model identifier and version
- Input data snapshot reference
- Confidence or uncertainty statement
- Human review requirement flag
- Plain-language rationale summary
- Appeal or override pathway

Recommendations lacking these elements may not be used for binding administrative decisions.

## Section 6 — Prohibited Uses

Automated systems may not make final determinations on biometric eligibility, electoral disqualification, or solitary procurement awards without human sign-off.
