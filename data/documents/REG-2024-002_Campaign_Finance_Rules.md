# Campaign Finance Rules (REG-2024-002)

## Article 15 — Disclosure Threshold for Campaign Expenses

Campaign expenses above **V$10,000** must be disclosed **weekly**. Each disclosure must include:

- Vendor name
- Expense category
- Amount
- Region
- Payment date

Late disclosures incur escalating penalties. Aggregate summaries must reconcile to bank statements submitted at filing deadlines.

## Article 16 — Prohibited Sources

Anonymous contributions above V$500 are prohibited. Foreign donations require prior registration and separate reporting.
