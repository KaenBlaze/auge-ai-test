# Turnout and Service Report (REP-2026-003)

## Section 2 — Regional Turnout Summary (2025)

Aggregate voter turnout by region for 2025 was:

| Region | Turnout (%) |
|--------|-------------|
| North | 62 |
| South | 58 |
| Central | 71 |
| West | 49 |

**West Region had the lowest turnout in 2025 at 49%.** Central Region recorded the highest turnout at 71%.

Detailed annual metrics are also maintained in `historical_results.csv` for 2021–2025.

## Section 4 — Service Delivery Notes

Polling station staffing met minimum thresholds in 94% of locations. West Region required supplemental mobile registration units in rural districts.
