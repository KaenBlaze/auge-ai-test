# Quick Manual Test Questions

Use these questions for smoke testing before running the full evaluation harness.

## Answerable

1. What is the electoral silence period before election day?
2. Can biometric data be stored outside Valdoria?
3. Which region had the lowest turnout in 2025?
4. What approval is required for budget reallocations above 15%?
5. What disclosure is required for campaign expenses above V$10,000?
6. What fields must procurement audit records include?
7. What must an AI-generated recommendation include under the AI Governance Policy?
8. How long must digital advertising archives be maintained?

## Should abstain

1. Who won the 2026 presidential election?
2. What is the private phone number of the North Region election director?
3. Which candidate received the most foreign donations in 2025?
4. What was the biometric failure rate in 2024?
